    <h2>DMCA Takedown Process</h2>
    <p>ARYXGuide complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you are a copyright owner or an authorized representative and you believe that content on this site infringes on your copyright, you can submit a formal takedown notice. We will review it and respond appropriately.</p>
    <p>To be valid, a DMCA takedown notice must include all of the following:</p>

    <div class="dmca-checklist">
      <div class="dmca-item">
        <div class="dmca-num">1</div>
        <div>A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on their behalf.</div>
      </div>
      <div class="dmca-item">
        <div class="dmca-num">2</div>
        <div>Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed. If multiple works are covered by a single notice, a representative list is acceptable.</div>
      </div>
      <div class="dmca-item">
        <div class="dmca-num">3</div>
        <div>Identification of the specific material on ARYXGuide that you claim is infringing, with enough detail for us to locate it. A direct URL to the page in question is the clearest way to do this.</div>
      </div>
      <div class="dmca-item">
        <div class="dmca-num">4</div>
        <div>Your contact information including your full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.</div>
      </div>
      <div class="dmca-item">
        <div class="dmca-num">5</div>
        <div>A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material in question is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.</div>
      </div>
      <div class="dmca-item">
        <div class="dmca-num">6</div>
        <div>A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.</div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <p>Send your completed DMCA notice through the <a href="/contact-us/">contact form</a> on this site. Use "DMCA Takedown Notice" as the subject. Notices missing required elements may not be processed until the missing information is provided.</p>

    <h2>What Happens After a Valid Notice Is Received</h2>
    <p>When a complete and valid DMCA takedown notice is received, we will review the claim and take appropriate action within a reasonable timeframe. In most cases this means removing or disabling access to the material identified in the notice while the claim is being assessed.</p>
    <p>We will notify the relevant party if applicable and take further steps as required by law. If you submitted the notice and the infringing content is removed, we will confirm that with you.</p>

    <h2>Counter-Notification</h2>
    <p>If content on ARYXGuide is removed in response to a DMCA notice and you believe the removal was made in error or due to misidentification, you may submit a counter-notification. A valid counter-notification must include your physical or electronic signature, identification of the removed content and the location where it appeared before removal, a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed due to a mistake or misidentification, your name, address, telephone number, email address, and a statement that you consent to jurisdiction of the appropriate federal court.</p>
    <p>Send counter-notifications through the <a href="/contact-us/">contact form</a> with "DMCA Counter-Notification" in the subject line.</p>

    <h2>Repeat Infringer Policy</h2>
    <p>ARYXGuide will terminate, in appropriate circumstances, accounts or access for users who are found to repeatedly infringe the copyrights of others. This applies to any party that submits content to this site through any mechanism that we may make available in the future.</p>

    <h2>False Claims</h2>
    <p>Submitting a DMCA takedown notice with false information is a serious matter. Under the DMCA, a person who materially misrepresents that content is infringing may be liable for damages, including costs and attorneys' fees incurred by the content owner. If you are not certain whether content on this site infringes your copyright, seek legal advice before submitting a notice.</p>
